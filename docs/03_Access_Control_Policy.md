# Access Control Policy
## Organization: [Company Name]
**Document Owner:** Identity and Access Management (IAM) Team
**Version:** 1.1

### 1. Purpose
To limit access to information systems and physical facilities to authorized users, processes, and devices.

### 2. General Access Control Guidelines
- **Principle of Least Privilege:** Users are granted the minimum level of access necessary to perform their job functions.
- **Role-Based Access Control (RBAC):** Access rights are tied to organizational roles rather than individual users.

### 3. Authentication Requirements
- **Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA):** Mandatory for all external access, administrative accounts, and access to core processing environments.
- **Password Standards:** Minimum of 14 characters, combining uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols. Passwords must not be reused.

### 4. Account Lifecycle Management
- **Provisioning:** HR and IT must coordinate to provision accounts upon onboarding.
- **Review:** Access rights for critical systems must be reviewed quarterly.
- **Deprovisioning:** Access must be revoked within 4 hours of an employee's or contractor's termination.
