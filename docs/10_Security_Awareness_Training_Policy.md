# Security Awareness Training Policy
## Organization: [Company Name]
**Document Owner:** CISO / Security Culture Team
**Version:** 1.1

### 1. Purpose
To ensure that all employees understand their role in protecting the organization against cyber threats such as social engineering, phishing, and insider threats.

### 2. Training Requirements
- **Onboarding:** All new hires must complete initial security awareness training within their first 14 days of employment.
- **Annual Refresh:** All staff must complete comprehensive security training annually.
- **Role-Specific:** Developers must undergo secure coding training annually; executives must undergo targeted spear-phishing and whaling awareness.

### 3. Phishing Simulations
- Unannounced phishing simulation campaigns will be conducted monthly.
- Employees who fail to report or fall victim to repeated simulations will be required to undergo mandatory remedial training.

### 4. Reporting
Metrics regarding training completion rates and phishing simulation performance will be reported to the executive board quarterly.
