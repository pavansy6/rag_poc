# Third-Party Risk Management Policy
## Organization: [Company Name]
**Document Owner:** Vendor Management / CISO
**Version:** 1.0

### 1. Purpose
To establish a framework for assessing, mitigating, and monitoring security risks associated with third-party vendors and service providers.

### 2. Vendor Assessment
- All prospective vendors handling sensitive data must undergo a security risk assessment prior to contract signing.
- Vendors must complete a Standardized Information Gathering (SIG) questionnaire.

### 3. Contractual Requirements
- **Right to Audit:** Contracts must include clauses permitting the organization to audit vendor security controls.
- **Breach Notification:** Vendors must be legally bound to notify the organization within 24 hours of discovering a data breach affecting corporate data.

### 4. Continuous Monitoring
High-risk vendors (e.g., cloud service providers) will be subjected to continuous risk monitoring and annual reassessments.
